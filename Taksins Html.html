About us
The Taksin
Syndicate story

Taksin Syndicate was founded on a simple idea: that Thailand's LPG energy market was fragmented, unreliable, and ripe for a better-run operator. Starting with a mini LPG filling factory in the Chanthaburi region, we built our capacity from the ground up.

Recognizing that production alone wasn't enough, we established Royal Chanthaburi Logistics (RCL) to take direct control of our distribution — ensuring our gas reached clients safely and on time, anywhere in Thailand.

The most recent chapter is Taksin Propane Gas (TPG) — our push into the retail market. With a strong production and logistics base already in place, TPG is positioned to offer local LPG shops and end consumers a more reliable and competitive alternative to incumbent suppliers.

Today, Taksin Syndicate operates as one of the region's most self-sufficient LPG energy groups — and we're just getting started.
